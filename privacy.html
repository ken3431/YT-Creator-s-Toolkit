Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 1, 2026

CreatorsToolkit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data within our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

Direct Answer: We do not collect or store personal identifiable information (PII) on our own servers.
  • YouTube Data: When you provide a YouTube URL or ID, this data is sent to our secure Cloudflare Worker to fetch public statistics via the YouTube Data API. We do not store your search history.
  • Images: Thumbnails uploaded for AI analysis are processed in real-time by Cloudflare Workers AI. These images are not saved after the analysis is complete.

2. Third-Party Services

Our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (Advertising)
  • Cloudflare (API Proxy & AI)

3. Advertising (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers to personalize ads and analyze traffic. You can opt-out of personalized advertising in your Android device settings.

4. YouTube API Client

CreatorsToolkit uses YouTube API Services. By using this app, users are agreeing to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy.

5. Data Safety (Google Play)

We declare that no user data is shared with third parties, except for the necessary technical data required by the service providers listed in Section 2.
