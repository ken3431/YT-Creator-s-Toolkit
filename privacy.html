Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Welcome to Creator's Toolkit for YT. This Privacy Policy governs your use of the mobile application developed by Annnon ("we," "us," or "our"). We operate under a strict "Privacy by Design" philosophy to ensure your data remains your own.

Summary for Creators We do not sell your data. We do not store your personal images or search history on our servers. We use minimal technical data only to provide the AI features and analytics tools found in the app.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Our app functions as a stateless utility. We categorize data usage into the following sectors:

1.1 YouTube Data API Services

Our app utilizes YouTube API Services to provide public channel metrics and tag extraction. When you input a URL or Handle:

  • The request is proxied through our secure Cloudflare Worker.
  • We fetch public metadata (tags, subscriber counts, monetization indicators).
  • No Personal Data: We do not access your private YouTube account or non-public video data.

1.2 Image Processing (AI Thumbnail Rater)

When using the AI Thumbnail Rater tool:

  • Ephemeral Processing: Images are transmitted via encrypted HTTPS to Cloudflare Workers AI for real-time analysis.
  • No Retention: Images are processed in volatile memory (RAM) and are permanently discarded immediately after results are generated.

1.3 Technical Device Information

To ensure stability, we may access non-identifying technical data such as device model, OS version, and network status (online/offline).

2. Third-Party Integrations

We integrate with industry-standard providers. Each handles data according to their own policies:

  • Google Play Services: Required for core functionality. View Policy
  • AdMob (Google): Used for advertisements. View Policy
  • Cloudflare: Provides infrastructure and AI processing. View Policy
  • YouTube API: Users are bound by the YouTube ToS and Google Privacy Policy.

3. Advertising and Choices

We use AdMob to keep this toolkit free. You can manage ad preferences in your Android device settings under Google > Ads by enabling "Opt out of interest-based ads."

4. Data Security

  • Encryption: All communication with our AI infrastructure is protected by 128-bit TLS/SSL encryption.
  • Anonymity: We do not share, rent, or sell any data to third-party data brokers.

5. Children’s Privacy

This App is intended for individuals aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If discovered, such data is immediately deleted.
