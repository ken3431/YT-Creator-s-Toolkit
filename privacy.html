Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Welcome to Creator's Toolkit for YT. This Privacy Policy governs your use of the mobile application developed by Annnon Software Development Services ("we," "us," or "our"). We take your digital privacy seriously and operate under a strict "Privacy by Design" philosophy.

Summary for Users: We do not sell your data. We do not store your personal images or search history on our servers. We use minimal technical data only to provide the AI features and analytics tools found in the app.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Our app is designed to function as a stateless utility. We categorize data usage into the following areas:

1.1 YouTube Data API Services

Our app utilizes YouTube API Services to provide public channel metrics and tag extraction. When you input a URL or Handle:

  • The request is proxied through our secure Cloudflare Worker.
  • We fetch public metadata (tags, subscriber counts, monetization indicators).
  • No Personal Data: We do not access your private YouTube account, login credentials, or non-public video data.

1.2 Image Processing (AI Thumbnail Rater)

When you use the AI Thumbnail Rater tool:

  • Ephemeral Processing: Images are transmitted via encrypted HTTPS to Cloudflare Workers AI for real-time visual analysis.
  • No Retention: Images are processed in volatile memory (RAM) and are permanently discarded immediately after the rating and tips are generated. We do not build databases of user-uploaded images.

1.3 Technical Device Information

To ensure app stability and fulfill advertising requirements, we may access non-identifying technical data such as device model, OS version, and network status (online/offline).

2. Third-Party Integrations & Legal Links

To provide our services, we integrate with industry-standard providers. Each provider handles data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: Required for app distribution and core functionality. View Policy
  • AdMob (Google): Used for monetization via personalized and non-personalized advertisements. View Policy
  • Cloudflare: Provides the serverless infrastructure (Workers) and AI processing power. View Policy
  • YouTube API: Users are bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.

3. Advertising and User Choice

We use Google AdMob to keep this app free for creators. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to provide a better experience. You can manage these preferences:

  • Open Google Settings on your Android device.
  • Select Ads.
  • Enable "Opt out of interest-based ads" or "Reset advertising ID."

4. Data Retention and Security

Because we do not use a centralized user database, there is no personal profile for us to "delete."

  • Security: All communication between the app and our AI infrastructure is protected by 128-bit TLS/SSL encryption.
  • Data Sharing: We do not share, rent, or sell any data to third-party data brokers.

5. Children’s Privacy

Creator's Toolkit for YT is intended for use by individuals aged 13 and older (or the legal age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

6. International Disclosures (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you have specific rights regarding data access and deletion. As we do not store PII, our "deletion" process is inherently fulfilled by our ephemeral processing model. For any specific inquiries regarding your rights, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
